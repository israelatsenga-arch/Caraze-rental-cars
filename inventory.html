Mercedes Benz

AMG G63

The G63 is powered by a 4.0-litre V8 biturbo engine producing 585 hp and 850 Nm of torque, paired with a starter generator for enhanced power delivery and responsiveness. It features twin-scroll turbochargers and AMG SPEEDSHIFT TCT 9G sports transmission, enabling rapid gear shifts without interrupting traction. The SUV accelerates from 0-60 mph in approximately 4.2 seconds despite its 5,842-pound curb weight. Three selectable differential locks and a ladder-type frame ensure exceptional off-road traction, while the AMG RIDE CONTROL suspension with adaptive damping balances on-road comfort.

S 580 Sedan

The S580 is powered by a 4.0-liter V8 biturbo engine paired with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system, producing 496 hp and 516 lb-ft of torque, allowing acceleration from 0–100 km/h in approximately 4.1–4.5 seconds with a top speed limited to 250 km/h. The 9G-TRONIC 9-speed automatic transmission powers all four wheels through the 4MATIC all-wheel-drive system, ensuring smooth power delivery and traction in various driving conditions. The AIRMATIC semi-active air suspension adapts ride height and dampening for comfort, aerodynamics, and stability.

Maybach Night Series S 680 4MATIC

The Mercedes-Maybach S 680 4MATIC is powered by a 6.0‑liter V12 biturbo engine (M279.980) producing 612 hp and 900 Nm of torque. With a SOHC valvetrain, three valves per cylinder, and multi‑port injection, it delivers smooth yet forceful performance. Thanks to its twin turbos and intercoolers, the sedan accelerates from 0–100 km/h in 4.5 seconds and reaches an electronically limited top speed of 250 km/h, combining effortless luxury with sports‑car levels of power.
